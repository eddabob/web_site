Description

I was inspired to write this composition when our small prep school had been closed down due to unbearable VATS, and I was only in Year 5. I thought of all the lost opportunities as a year 6 for my final year in my treasured school. So the feeling of loss for me and my friends was overwhelming and bittersweet. So I wanted to express this through music- as that was, for me, the strongest way to do it- and so I wrote "We are together forever" to express the bond we had as a class; now, I thought that a short extract of this piece would be a fitting ringtone to remind us of our friendship and the good times we had together.
